Format

Enter separate individuals/haplotypes on separate lines. Commas (,) and semi-colons (;) within the line are ok (will be ignored).

  • Motif Only (see example)
  • Name and Motif (see example)
  • Name, N, and Motif (see example)

Output

  • HVR1 (positions 16024-16365)
  • HVR2 (positions 73-340)
  • HVR1 and HVR2 (positions 16024-16365 and 73-340)
  • HVR1 through HVR2 (positions 16024-16569 and 1-340)
  • Coding Region (positions 577-15992)
  • All (positions 1-16569)

To retrieve the revised Cambridge Reference Sequence, use 'rCRS' as the motif.
